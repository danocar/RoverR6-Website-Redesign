About us

Hi, we are Mark & Angie Gray and MGBD Parts is the part of our business dedicated to keeping Rover P6 cars in all variants, 2000, 2200 & 3500 both as everyday and show condition cars. Our current fleet includes an everyday user 1966 Rover 2000, and also a 1972 Rover 2000TC export, 1975 Rover 2200 Auto, 1972 Rover 3500 Estoura (estate) and of course, the last P6 off the production line 'VVC 700S', the avocado Rover 3500S.

We have had over 25 years involvement in classic car clubs mostly at committee level and have now turned our specialist knowledge and experience into our Rover P6 part business 'MGBD Parts', which is deicated to suppling quality parts for all variants of the Rover P6 built between 1963-1977. The parts stocked are predominantly brand new manufactured items but there is also a mix of 'NOS' (new old stock) , refurbished service exchange and second hand parts. But if you cant see what you need place email or call us. Also if you need any advice or technical help, we are only a phone call away and we'll be glad to help and what's more, that's free!

Please not that all refurbished, NOS and Secondhand parts are detailed as such in the webstore, and therefore anything else listed is a brand new manufactured item. You can click on the catergories list on the left of the webstore or use the product search facility to look through our items by description or by part number. To make life easier for you we have listed all of the parts by the original Rover parts numbers that were used in the Rover Parts catalogues, so you can look your part up by its Rover number.
